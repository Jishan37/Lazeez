#Background


Both my mother and I share a deep passion for cooking. In the midst of the pandemic, 2021, while lockdown confined us, I seized the opportunity to transform our shared passion for cooking into something special. I harnessed our shared love for cooking, creating Lazeez—a Facebook page where customers could savor the delectable dishes crafted by my talented mother. Her culinary prowess, evident in the diverse array of dishes she prepares, has been the heart of Lazeez's success.


Lazeez stands as a beacon—a testament to the transformative power of culinary passion and the indomitable spirit that can turn a shared love for cooking into something truly extraordinary.


Lazeez has become more than just a platform; it is a testament to the resilience of passion, transforming adversity into an opportunity to share the richness of our culinary heritage.
